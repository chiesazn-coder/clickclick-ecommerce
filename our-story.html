From a simple need to a daily essential

We loved the quality of the rear camera, but framing was a guessing game. External monitors were bulky and overkill for everyday content.

We asked: what if a screen could just… click into place? Prototyping began with creators, small businesses, and parents. Each iteration got thinner, lighter, and more stable.
